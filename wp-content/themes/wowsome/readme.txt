=== WOWSOME ===

Wowsome is a Multipurpose Creative, Simple, Clean, Professional, Beautiful Free WordPress Theme. It is easily customizable with customize. Some of the highlighted options are two site layout (Narrow and Wide), 4 layouts for every post/page, Business Page Templates, Advance Widgets, Featured slider to highlight your post, Social Profile Navigation, Infinite Scroll and many more. Supports popular plugins like Breadcrumb NavXT, WP-PageNavi and Contact Form 7. It is also translation ready. Get free support at https://www.themehorse.com/support-forum/ and View beautiful demo site at https://www.themehorse.com/preview/wowsome
